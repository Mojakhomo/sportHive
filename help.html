How Can We Help You?

Welcome to the SportHive Help Center. Find answers to common questions or contact our support team for personalized assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I place a bet on SportHive?

    To place a bet, first create an account and log in. Navigate to the sport you're interested in, select the match, and choose your betting options. Add your selections to the bet slip, enter your stake, and confirm your bet.

  • How can I purchase team merchandise?

    Visit our Merchandise section, browse through our collection of official team gear, select your items, and proceed to checkout. We offer worldwide shipping and various payment options.

  • Where can I watch live matches?

    Click on the sport of your choice from the home page to see all available live matches. Some matches may require a premium subscription or pay-per-view access.

  • How do I reset my password?

    Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. The link will expire in 24 hours for security reasons.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and various e-wallets. For betting, we also accept cryptocurrency payments.
